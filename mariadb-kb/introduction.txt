================================
MARIADB INTRODUCTION
================================

MariaDB Innodb is called XtraDB

MariaDB Cluster is called Galera (this is NOT ndbcluster)
  This is not ndbcluster equivalent
  This is replication based cluster
  There are no longer separate MariaDB Galera Cluster releases for MariaDB 10.1 and above. 
  Simply download MariaDB (10.1 or above) and configure your cluster as normal.
