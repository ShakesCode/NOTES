ECLIPSE ARCHETYPES

Upon installation and first use of Eclipse, archetypes may not be available in local repos.

Add a remote repo:
Menu --> Eclipse --> Preferences --> Maven 
          --> Archetypes 
          --> Add Remote Catalog http://repo1.maven.org/maven2/archetype-catalog.xml
                  or http://repo.maven.apache.org/maven2/archetype-catalog.xml
                  (from http://maven.apache.org/archetype/maven-archetype-plugin/specification/archetype-catalog.html)
                  
The local repo:
Local repo is in ~/.m2 --> ~/.m2/archetype-catalog.xml
