- To start the service:
$ ruby <filename>

Like:
$ ruby hello.rb

- To access the service:
http://localhost:4567

- EXAMPLE 1

$ cat hello.rb
require 'sinatra'

get "/" do
  "Hello World!"
end

- EXAMPLE 2

$ cat filetouch.rb
require 'sinatra'
require 'fileutils'

get "/" do

  FileUtils.touch('/tmp/file.txt')
  "Hello World!"

end
